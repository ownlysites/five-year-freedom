FAQ

Common questions. Honest answers.

Why is this 1:1 only? Why no group cohort tier?
Because the outcome promises — first deal closed, cashflow in 90 days, full Triple-Tax Promise — only happen with hands-on mentorship. A group call once a week can't get you to a closed deal. Direct 1:1 with me, every week, can. That's why I cap the cohort at 12 active members.
What if I'm not in real estate? Is this for me?
Yes — real estate agents are the primary persona because the Income Snowball + REACH + UFF stack was built for them, but business owners and family-builders thrive in the Inner Circle too. The roadmap adapts: agents get the agent-flagship deliverables, business owners get the capital-extraction + first-investment-property build, and family-builders get the household-wide Triple-Tax Promise + first investment property as a foundation. Same 12-month engineering, different starting point.
What does the “first real estate deal in 90 days” actually look like?
We start sourcing in Week 1. By Week 6 we've identified 2-3 viable properties, pre-qualified financing (likely with our partner Truss Financial Group for cross-border or DSCR cases), structured the entity, and begun due diligence. By Week 10-12 you're at the closing table. I'm with you on every call, every offer, every negotiation. You execute — I'm the seat-belt.
What's the “cashflow in 90 days or we extend free” guarantee?
If, by Day 90 of your engagement, you haven't generated at least one new monthly cashflow stream — whether from the first real estate deal, a residual program (REACH, UFF, etc.), or capital extracted from your existing assets — we extend the engagement at no additional charge until it's live. The promise isn't paid in money; it's paid in time and direct attention. You don't lose, period.
Is the $24,997 negotiable? Can I just buy month-to-month?
Pricing isn't negotiable — every member pays the same. The two payment paths are paid-in-full ($24,997) or zero-interest monthly ($2,497 × 12 with credit approval). There's no month-to-month option because the program is engineered as a 12-month build; quitting in month 4 means quitting before the system compounds.
What if I apply and don't get accepted?
You get an honest answer about why and a recommendation for the right next step inside the Ownly ONCE ecosystem — usually a free 6 Figure Sit Down, a WealthPath Freedom Score, or one of the partner programs (Tax Hive, ARF, etc.) that fits your moment. No hard feelings, no waste of your time.
Are you a fiduciary / RIA / licensed advisor?
I'm a Certified Financial Education Instructor (CFEI) and a Professional Financial Services Association (PFSA) member. I'm not a Registered Investment Advisor (RIA), and I don't manage portfolios or recommend specific securities. The Inner Circle is education, mentorship, and implementation alongside vetted partners (CPA-led tax strategy, real estate brokerages, lenders, etc.) — each acting in their licensed capacity. Anything that requires fiduciary advice gets routed through the right partner.
How fast can I start?
If accepted, we typically kick off within 7-14 days of the discovery call — the time to do paperwork, payment, intake survey, and schedule your first weekly 1:1. We also activate WealthPath Premium and the DJ AI Coach immediately on Day 1.
